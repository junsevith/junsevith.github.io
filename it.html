My (Computer) Science interests

Throughout my life I've tried many programming languages like: Python, C, C++, Java, Kotlin, ABAP, Ada, Prolog, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP but for a while my absolute favourite is Rust.

I like that it is low level, yet modern, robust and safe, yet flexible and powerful and most importantly really fast

When I program I focus on the performance and good composition of the code. I enjoy implementing interesting ideas and using novel approaches to problems. I take tremendous joy form programs I consider good, and that perfectionism is what motivates my work the most. I also like a good challenge, and overcoming difficult problems gives me a lot of satisfaction.

I took quite a liking to Discrete Optimization it allows for an elegant approach for solving optimization programs in many fields. I especially enjoyed programming directed approaches like graph traversion and network-flow problems but linear programming being as universal as it is, was my favourite thing.

I actually used Julia with JuMP solver to solve a puzzle I had encountered in a video game. It was quite a challenge to model something so complex, but I was successful and found a solution better than any other I've encountered.
